The author is a lecturer in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics, Laikipia University. Previously, he was a graduate assistant and also a tutorial fellow at the same Department. Daniel holds bachelor's, master's, and PhD degrees in Statistics. His primary field of research is in inferential statistics, especially in multiple testing. Daniel also has an interest in statistical learning, genetics research, and consulting. Daniel was the best graduating student from the School of Science and Applied Technology (SSAT) in 2015. In his free time, Daniel is passionate about geopolitics, history, and football.


Research Interests
======
- Multiple Testing
- Statistical Learning
